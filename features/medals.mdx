## Overview

The medal system on ScoreSaber Reloaded rewards players for achieving top positions on ranked map leaderboards. Medals provide an alternative competitive metric beyond PP, emphasizing consistency and competitive placement across many maps.

## Medal System

Medals are awarded based on leaderboard position:

### Medal Values by Rank

<Accordion title="Medal Allocation">
  - **Rank #1** - 10 medals (Gold, first place)
  - **Rank #2** - 8 medals (Silver, second place)
  - **Rank #3** - 6 medals (Bronze, third place)
  - **Rank #4** - 5 medals
  - **Rank #5** - 4 medals
  - **Rank #6** - 3 medals
  - **Rank #7** - 2 medals
  - **Rank #8** - 1 medal
  - **Rank #9** - 1 medal
  - **Rank #10** - 1 medal
</Accordion>

<Note>
  Medals are only awarded for **ranked** maps. Unranked maps do not contribute to medal counts.
</Note>

### Medal Calculation

Your total medal count is calculated by:

1. Finding your highest position on each ranked leaderboard
2. Awarding medals based on the position (using the table above)
3. Summing all medals across all maps
4. Updating in real-time as you set new scores or other players beat your scores

<Tip>
  Unlike PP, medals don't use a weighted curve. Every top-10 position contributes fully to your medal total, regardless of map difficulty.
</Tip>

## Medal Hunting

Strategies to increase your medal count:

### Finding Medal Opportunities

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Target Easy Wins">
    - Search for low-play-count maps where top 10 is achievable
    - Filter maps by star range matching your skill level
    - Focus on newly ranked maps before competition intensifies
    - Check maps in your top PP range (you already excel at that difficulty)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Protect Existing Medals">
    - Monitor your medal scores for players approaching your position
    - Set up alerts (future feature) when you drop out of top 10
    - Revisit maps where you hold #4-#10 to improve to top 3
    - Focus on maps with high medal values (#1-#3)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Strategic Grinding">
    - Create a playlist of maps where you're ranked #11-#20
    - Sort by maps with the most medals at stake (aim for #1-#3)
    - Practice consistently on these maps
    - Replay when you're performing at your peak
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  Medals favor **breadth** over **depth**. Setting 100 scores in the top 10 earns more medals than perfecting 10 scores to #1.
</Tip>

## Medal vs PP Rankings

Understanding the differences:

### PP Rankings

- **Weighted System** - Top scores count most, weighted curve
- **Difficulty Focus** - Higher star maps give more PP
- **Accuracy Sensitive** - Small accuracy differences = large PP gaps
- **Top-Heavy** - Your best 50-100 scores determine PP

### Medal Rankings

- **Unweighted System** - All top-10 scores count equally
- **Consistency Focus** - Rewards breadth across many maps
- **Position-Based** - Rank on leaderboard matters more than score/accuracy
- **Inclusive** - Easy and hard maps contribute equally

<Accordion title="Which Ranking Should I Focus On?">
  **Focus on PP if:**
  - You want to be highly ranked in the global leaderboards
  - You enjoy pushing yourself on challenging maps
  - You prefer depth over breadth
  - Accuracy optimization is your strength

  **Focus on Medals if:**
  - You enjoy competitive leaderboard battles
  - You prefer playing a wide variety of maps
  - You want an alternative to PP grinding
  - You like tracking incremental progress

  **Do Both:**
  - Most players naturally earn medals while grinding PP
  - Medal hunting is a great secondary goal
  - Diversifies your skill development
</Accordion>

## Medal API Integration

Access medal data programmatically:

### Get Medal Rankings

```http
GET /ranking/medals/{page}?country={countryCode}
```

**Parameters:**
- `page` (number) - Page number (1-indexed)
- `country` (optional string) - ISO 3166-1 alpha-2 country code

**Response:**
```json
{
  "items": [
    {
      "id": "player_id",
      "name": "Player Name",
      "country": "US",
      "medals": 1523,
      "medalsRank": 42,
      "countryMedalsRank": 7
    }
  ],
  "metadata": {
    "totalItems": 50000,
    "itemsPerPage": 50,
    "page": 1
  },
  "countryMetadata": {
    "US": 5234,
    "UK": 3421,
    ...
  }
}
```

### Get Player Medal Stats

Included in standard player profile endpoint:

```http
GET /player/{playerId}
```

Response includes:
- `medals` (number) - Total medal count
- `medalsRank` (number) - Global medal rank
- `countryMedalsRank` (number) - Country medal rank

<Note>
  Medal data is updated in real-time as scores are submitted. Rankings recalculate every 5 minutes.
</Note>

## Tips for Medal Ranking

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Efficient Medal Farming">

    - Target the 4-6 star range where competition is moderate

    - Diversify across all star ranges for maximum opportunities
    - Track your medal/day rate to monitor progress
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Maintaining Medal Position">

    - Revisit maps where you've dropped from #10 to #11-#15
